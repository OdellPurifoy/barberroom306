About

Voted “Top Three” for barbershops in NYC from Business Insider, Barber Room 306 has been creating giving expert-level cuts since it’s opening in 2014. A hip Williamsburg vibe, they offer squeaky-clean fades and flawless scissor-work hairstyles. They focus on the things that matter; like quality, precision and top notch service. Check out our feature in Fractal’s New York: Barber Shop Tour. Their staff consists of talented barbers, all perfectionists with their work, fine-tuning each haircut with a straight razor after wrapping a hot towel around your neck. While you wait, feel free to enjoy a beer, a shot or an espresso. An eclectic mix of classical rock, jazz, deep house and some nineties hip hop plays in the background, and the new shop dog, Buffy, sleeps quietly in the corner. Barber Room 306 takes pride in their work, and consider barbering the not just a time-honored craft, but a true art form.
